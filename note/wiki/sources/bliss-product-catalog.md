# The Bliss Product Catalog

Comprehensive product documentation for The Bliss at Home signature massage and spa products. 6-page Thai/English catalog detailing natural therapeutic formulations.

## Product Portfolio

### 1. Foot Massage Cream (ผลิตภัณฑ์สำหรับนวดเท้า)
**Deep Restorative Care** for feet that bear weight all day
- **Base**: Rice Bran Oil + Shea Butter
- **Benefits**: Repairs dry/cracked feet, long-lasting moisture
- **Formulation**: Designed for reflexology pressure point work

### 2. Signature Body Oil (The Bliss Signature Body Oil)
**3-oil therapeutic blend** for full-body massage
- **Rice Bran Oil**: Antioxidant power (Gamma Oryzanol), lightweight
- **Jojoba Oil**: Mimics human sebum, balances skin oils, non-comedogenic  
- **MCT Oil**: Smooth glide, antibacterial, odorless, no residue

### 3. Aromatherapy Oil "Bliss Me"
**Essential oil therapy** for mind-body relaxation
- **Lavender**: Nervous system calming, stress reduction, better sleep
- **Rosemary**: Energizing, muscle tension relief, circulation boost
- **Premium base oils**: Same 3-oil foundation as body oil

### 4. Herbal Therapeutic Balm
**100% natural Thai herb therapy** for deep muscle relief
- **เถาอุปรียง (Jewel Vine)**: Pain relief, inflammation reduction
- **เถาอื่นอ่อน (Tendon Specialist)**: Tendon flexibility, joint mobility
- **ว่านหางจระเข้ (Aloe Vera)**: Cooling, anti-inflammatory
- **No synthetic chemicals**: "เส้น กล้ามเนื้อ และผิวพรรณ" care in one product

## Core Ingredient Science

### Rice Bran Oil (น้ำมันรำข้าว)
```
Gamma Oryzanol: High antioxidant compound
Vitamin E: Free radical protection  
Fast absorption: Non-greasy penetration
Natural radiance: Skin brightening effect
```

### Jojoba Oil (น้ำมันโจโจบา)  
```
Sebum mimicry: 96% structural similarity to human skin oils
Moisture barrier: Long-term hydration without pore clogging
pH balance: Regulates oily/dry skin naturally
Shelf stable: Doesn't go rancid (technically a wax ester)
```

### MCT Oil (น้ำมันมะพร้าวสกัดบริสุทธิ์)
```
Medium-chain triglycerides: C8-C12 fatty acids
Antibacterial: Natural antimicrobial properties
Clean finish: No sticky residue on skin or linens
Therapist-friendly: Smooth glide for extended massage
```
